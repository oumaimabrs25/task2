Top 5 Luxury Bags Brands

Every season, new handbag trends make their way into the street style world, and each season, we eagerly scout said accessories. Over fashion weeks and general street spotting,the fashion crowd has shown that as far as handbags go, there are a few that never go out of style.

  1. The Birkin Bag


    2. Named after Jane Birkin, this bag has been coveted since its inception in 1984. The BBC revealed how it came to be: "Its existence was a happy accident, borne out of an exchange between Birkin and former Hermès chief executive Jean-Louis Dumas on an Air France flight from Paris to London in the early '80s," the BBC wrote.


  2. The Chanel Quilted Flap Bag


    3. Nothing says luxury like a Chanel bag. The quilted flap designs have been an integral part of the brand's history since Coco Chanel herself designed this bag in 1955.

  3. The Mulberry Alexa


    4. Having launched at the very start of the 2010s, Mulberry's Alexa bag—which was, of course, named after Alexa Chung—became an instant It bag. If you didn't have one, you wanted one.

  4. The Gucci Jackie


    5. A handbag roundup is not complete without mentioning Gucci, and the brand's Jackie bag has become a classic. If you recognise the name, then it's probably because, technically, this bag isn't new.

  5. The Dior Saddle Bag

    6. Dior's first Saddle Bag debuted in 1999 when John Galliano was still at the helm of the house, and its high resale value is proof that it's still coveted today. Rosie Huntington-Whiteley and Rihanna are among the celebrities who own this chic style.
